Clinical Intake Assistant

This demo showcases a sleep intake workflow, but the underlying engine is designed to support multi‑department clinical intake across cardiology, pulmonary, neurology, pain management, behavioral health, and more.

In our service area, more than 1 in 5 adults struggle with low literacy, which makes traditional medical forms difficult to understand.

To support these patients, this assistant includes Read‑Aloud and Voice Input options.

You may click “What do these buttons do?” to learn the controls, or click Start Demo Session to begin.
