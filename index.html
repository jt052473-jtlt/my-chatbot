Clinical Intake Assistant Prototype

This demo showcases a sleep intake workflow, but the underlying engine is designed to support multi‑department clinical intake across cardiology, pulmonary, neurology, pain management, behavioral health, and more.

The goal of this prototype is to demonstrate how conversational AI can streamline patient intake, reduce staff workload, and improve data quality before the patient ever reaches the clinic.
